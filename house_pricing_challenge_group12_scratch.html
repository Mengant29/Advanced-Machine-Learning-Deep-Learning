House Pricing Prediction¶

This challenge is adapted from the well-known 'Zillow's Home Value Prediction' competition on Kaggle. In particular, given a dataset containing descriptions of homes on the US property market, your task is to make predictions on the selling price of as-yet unlisted properties. Developing a model which accurately fits the available training data while also generalising to unseen data-points is a multi-faceted challenge that involves a mixture of data exploration, pre-processing, model selection, and performance evaluation.

Hyperparameter tuning¶

Theory about gradient boosting:

Boosting is a method of converting weak learners into a strong learner ensemble. In boosting, each new tree is a fit on a modified version of the original data set.

After evaluating the first tree, we increase the weights of those observations that are difficult to classify and lower the weights for those that are easy to classify.
